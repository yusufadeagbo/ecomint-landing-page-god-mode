Frequently Asked Questions

How does EcoMint pricing work?

Pricing is transparent and varies by location and waste type. You'll see the exact cost before confirming your pickup. No hidden fees.

Is my area covered?

We currently serve Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt. Check our coverage map above or download the app to see if we're in your neighborhood.

How do I become an operator?

Visit our Operators page to start your free trial. You'll need to verify your business and set up your coverage area before activating agents.

What is Eco QR?

Eco QR is our secure payment verification system. It ensures every pickup is properly recorded and paid for, protecting both collectors and customers.
